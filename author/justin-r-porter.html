LLEMR

is a Lightweight EMR.

it's free, it's open source, and it's designed for free clinics

Focused

LLEMR avoids the bloat of commercial alternatives by focusing. LLEMR is so intuitive that new users don't usually need training.

Open Source

LLEMR is open source software, which means it's usually less expensive than commercial alternatives and, more importantly, it puts the power in your hands.

Feature-rich

LLEMR is a collaboration of multiple clinics using many workflows. We probably already have whatever features you need.
